My first holiday

About Your Holiday

Christmas is a holiday where you spend time with your family and have meals together, you give gifts to your loved ones and anyone in need. There are many fun activities to do during Christmas time and you get to decorate your house with your family.

Your Special Holiday Meaning

Christmas time is special to me because it was my first US holiday ever, we celebrated it about a month after we landed here and everything was so pretty, there were lights and trees everywhere and we didn't celebrate chrismas like this in Dubai so it was very new to me and at first I didn't understand the point of the gifts, trees and all the lights but over the past 3 years it's become my favorite time of the year.
